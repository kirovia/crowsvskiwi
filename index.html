CROWS vs KIWI
Welcome to Crows vs Kiwi! To play, click a piece and move it to a highlighted space. A piece must move as far as it can. To win, you must get all four of your pieces into one of four winning positions:


Four in a row (horizontally or vertically)






Diagonally (in the opposite of your starting orientation)




Four in a square (anywhere on the board)








Four corners
